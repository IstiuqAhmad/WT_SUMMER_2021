How to Register For Bangladesh Railway Online Ticket In Esheba
<br>
<br>
Step 1:
First of all, you have to register on the Bangladesh Railway Esheba website. 
You will not be able to buy a ticket before register on the Railway Esheba website. 
Let’s check out the instructions.
<br>
<br>
Visit this website (https://www.esheba.cnsbd.com/).
You will enter the Bangladesh Railway Esheba website. 
There you will see a “User Sign In” form on the right side of the webpage.
To register, click on  “Sign Up” first.
<br>
<br>
Step 2:
By clicking “Sign Up,” you will enter a register webpage like the image below. 
You have you fill-up the registration form with valid information. 
Then Click on “Register.”
<br>
<br>
Step 3:
After registration, you will get a verification mail from Bangladesh Railway. 
In that mail, they will give you a verification link. By clicking this link, 
your registration will be complete.
